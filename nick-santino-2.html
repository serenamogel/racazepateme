Career

Nicholas Bernard Santino is a singer-songwriter and musician from the United States. He is best known for being the lead singer of the pop-rock band A Rocket to the Moon. He moved on to pursue a solo career after the band disbanded in 2013. He’s released four solo EPs as well as a full-length album, Big Skies, on May 27, 2014. He formed the pop-rock band Beach Weather in 2015, and the band issued two EPs: What a Drag and Chit Chat.
