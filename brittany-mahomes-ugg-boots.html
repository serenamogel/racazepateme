Shop Brittany Mahomes' Favorite UGG Boots 2023

June 2024 · 2 minute read

It’s finally time to bring out those UGG boots that have been collecting dust in the back of your closet—because Brittany Mahomes just did. The former soccer athlete, married to NFL Kansas City Chiefs player Patrick Mahomes, fell into the October mood in her latest Instagram story. The picture, which can be seen on FootWear News, featured her two kids in festive fall outfits. *Cue us running to grab our cozy sweaters and a pumpkin spice latte*

Mahomes and her two-year-old daughter, Sterling Skye, wore matching UGG boots that ignited all kinds of 2000s fashion nostalgia. You can join in on the seasonal fun by purchasing a pair of your own UGGS—especially if you dumped them or donated them when they fell out of style. Shop the Classic Short II in the color chestnut for $180—available in women's, men's, and kids' sizes.

The bestseller shoe comes in ten other colors to pair with every fall flannel imaginable, like the traditional black, mustard seed, navy, and grey. Mahomes' son, Patrick, was seen sporting some super comfy socks and a cute onesie (we would love a pair of those winter socks, too, TBH). The entrepreneur coordinated her chestnut UGGS with maroon-adjacent sweatpants and a sweatshirt, while her daughter, Sterling, wore an adorable knitted cardigan and modern light-wash jeans. Who knew a toddler was more fashionable than us or that we’d be re-purchasing UGGS that we never thought would make a comeback? Not us, that’s for sure.
