MLB The Show 23 Community Market Perks and More

September 2024 Â· 5 minute read

MLB The Show 23 community market involves the buying and selling of players. You can also buy trade equipment and stadium in the game.

The latest version of the baseball video game by San Diego Studio based on Major League Baseball was recently released on March 28, 2023.

One can play and enjoy this game on the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and Nintendo Switch. The Xbox versions of the game are available for Xbox Game Pass subscribers at no additional cost.

The game cover features the Miami Marlins second baseman and center fielder Jazz Chisholm Jr. followed by the former New York Yankees shortstop Derek Jeter in its "The Captain Edition."

Besides being one of the most played live-in MLB video games, The Show 23's new gameplays and trading ways have intrigued online users.

MLB The Show 23 Sell Players In Community Market

Players are purchased and sold off by the use of Stubs, the online currency of the game in The Show 23. The cost is updated every 30 seconds.

The market price is variable, given the demand for the player cards. Also, the buying price of one player is widely different than its selling price.

This depicts that one has to thoroughly check the prices and make a smart move to sell later. Evan Longoria is currently one of the most expensive players in the game, nearly reaching 300k stubs.

MLB The Show 23 Perks

Speed, power and contact are the best perks in MLB The Show 23 video game. These are used to enhance the performance of players.

While new Perks are awarded as one progresses through Skill Set Programs in the game, Perks can also be purchased and sold with Stubs in the community market. Various types of perks come with great benefits as well as a disadvantage.

Perks available in the market are:

  • Break
  • Contact
  • Control
  • Fielding
  • Power
  • Speed
  • Velocity

The perks can be identified by their type and their number variations. The highest buying price comes to 3000 stubs, while the high selling price is one thousand lower than the buying one.

Push Square confirms that Each Perk type has a pro and a con. Hence, a Power Perk may boost a player's overall Power but decreases fielding.

Likewise, Perks also come in different categories in terms of their working capacity. The four categories of Perks are:

  • Bronze
  • Silver
  • Gold
  • Diamond

While the Bronze Perks are the easiest to unlock and a much cheaper price for buying, the price and gaining process equally become harder by each category.

The diamond perks are the most expensive ones and the hardest to unlock. However, they also give the biggest statistical bonuses, which are worth the price one pays.

MLB The Show 23 Stadium Trade

The option to create a stadium is available on the main menu of MLB The Show 23, which opens a new section called "myStadiums."

A user can create a baseball stadium out of their own creativity and name them on the basis of their likability. Unlike players, the stadium can be purchased on fewer Stubs, and the same cycle follows while selling them. Most of the stadiums are listed at 1200 Stubs, followed by 1199 Stubs.

As the trading of such stadiums is possible and ongoing in the Show 23 community market, one cannot transfer or regain their previous stadium accomplishments from Show 22.

The new game only allows us to build a new stadium and start a new game, unlike the presence of the previous year's saves. The new generation game is solely based on the fruition of this game.
