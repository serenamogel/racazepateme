Penn Badgley | Career

In 1999 and 2000, Badgley did voice work for the video games Mario Golf 64′ and ‘Mario Tennis 64.’

He made his screen debut on an episode of Will & Grace.

From 2000 to 2001, he was known for his role as Phillip Chancellor IV on the soap opera ‘The Young and the Restless.’

For his work, he was nominated for a Young Artist Award in 2001 for Best Performance in a Daytime Series.

The following year, he starred as Joel Larsen in The WB’s comedy-drama series ‘Do-Over,’ in which he played a 34-year-old man.

Badgley’s big break came when he was cast as Dan Humphrey in the CW’s teen drama series “Gossip Girl” in 2007.

In 2009, he played the stepson of a serial killer in the thriller The Stepfather, a remake of the 1987 original.

In ‘Easy A,’ Badgley portrayed Todd, the romantic interest of the main character Olive (Emma Stone).
