Professional Career of Otar Saralidze

Otar Saralidze began his career as a model before moving into the entertainment world. Then he worked as a dubbing artist. Dakota from Milo Murphy's Law is one of the many characters he has voiced in television series and movies.

He eventually moved into acting, starring in the Polish television series Colors Of Happiness. He then debuted in the film industry in the year 2011 with a Georgian series named 1920. 

Saralidze went on to star in the film Foreign Body in 2014, which received positive feedback. His popularity skyrocketed after he starred as Domenico in the sexual romance film 365 Days in 2020.
