Ty Gibbs Age, Net Worth, Height, Wins, Car, Father

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Ty Gibbs is an American professional stock car racing driver. He was born on October 4, 2002. He competes full-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, driving the No, 54 Toyota Supra for Joe Gibbs Racing.

Gibbs began running Shifter Karts at GoPro Motorplex in the summer of 2015 at the age of 13. In January 2021, JGR announced that Gibbs would make his Xfinity Series debut in 2021 at the Daytona Road Course, which would be the first race of a part-time schedule he would run in the team’s No. 54 car.

For 2022, Gibbs moved into the series full-time, driving the No.54.
